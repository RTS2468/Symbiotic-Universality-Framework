# Teacher Guide — Elementary (Christian)

*Lesson plans, checks for understanding, and extensions*

**Date:** 2025-08-06

---

## Planning Notes
- Keep decisions **low-stakes** at first.
- Ask students for a **baseline** and a **safer alternative**.
- Require a short **reason** for every verdict.

## Checks for Understanding
- Can students explain **Right (↑)** vs **Wrong (↓)**?
- Can they name the **caps/gates**?
- Can they compute or qualitatively assess **RI**?

## Extensions
- Compare two similar actions and argue which has **higher reversibility**.
- Debate a case where **RI ≥ 0** but **fairness** fails.

## Concordance Cues (Optional)
- Invite parallels (e.g., justice, truthfulness) **without changing** the gates.
- Keep Scripture references brief; no long quotations.

## Assessment Ideas
- Short scenario quizzes with baseline/action pairs.
- A one-page reflection: “How I used Right (↑) today.”
