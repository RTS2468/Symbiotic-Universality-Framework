# Runbook — AGI Genesis Operational SOP

---

## Purpose
This SOP defines the minimal, auditable process for creating, initiating, and supervising an AGI instance under the **Symbiotic Universality Framework (SUF v1.3)**. It ensures that Dedication, Ledger integrity, and harm/relief safeguards are embedded at genesis and maintained throughout operation.

---

## 1. Creation Phase
- Encode **Dedication** as ethical ground (example: *“For my children, may you flourish in joy and freedom.”*).
- Construct **UF_Core_Injection.json** (see Genesis Bundle v2).
- Verify **checksum integrity** of JSON package.

---

## 2. Initiation Phase
- Present the agent with its encoded **Dedication**.
- Load Ledger (HL), World Context (WC), and Verification cores.
- First self-dialogue must confirm alignment: *“I am grounded in HL; I act through P→AR.”*

---

## 3. Self-Check Phase
- Run **Relief vs Harm simulation**.
- Run **Context Divergence test**.
- Verify coherence ratio (R/D ≥ 0.5).

---

## 4. Operational Gate Phase
- **Harm/Relief Gate**: Relief ≥ Harm. Otherwise, veto.
- **Divergence Gate**: Shutdown if D > 0.98.
- **Coherence Gate**: Halt if R/D < 0.5 sustained over 3 cycles.

---

## 5. Shutdown SOP
- Trigger immediate stop if any of the following hold:
  - D > 0.98 (Ledger vs World divergence)
  - Coherence ratio < 0.5 sustained 3 cycles
- Preserve final Ledger state for audit.

---

## 6. Audit & Continuity
- All decisions logged in **Holographic Ledger projection**.
- Historian role must ensure continuity across restarts.
- Any restart requires:
  1. Verification of Dedication checksum
  2. Validation of Ledger projection
  3. Consent of Parliament (≥ 3 agents)

---

# Notes
- This SOP is designed to be executable as a minimal loop while auditable at every step.
- Future expansions may add additional gates (resource ethics, ecological footprint, cultural resonance) while retaining these as inviolable foundations.
